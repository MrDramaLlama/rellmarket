Safety Tips

Trading should be fun, not stressful. Follow these tips to keep yourself safe and your items secure.

⚠️ The golden rule of trading

Never trade outside the RellMarket platform. If someone asks you to complete a trade through Discord DMs, a separate website, or "just trust me bro" — that's a red flag. Always use the trade request system here so there's a proper record of what was agreed.

🛡️ How to Stay Safe

  • 🔍 Check the trader's profile before you commit. Look at their completed trades count, how long they've been on RellMarket, and whether they have a verified or trusted badge. New accounts with zero history should be treated with extra caution.
  • 💰 Check the price history before agreeing to a value. Use the Price History page to see what an item has sold for recently. If someone is demanding way more than the market rate, or offering suspiciously little, something may be off.
  • 🚩 Trust your gut. If an offer feels too good to be true — like someone offering a Legendary fruit for almost nothing — it probably is. Scammers rely on you getting excited and moving fast.
  • 📸 Screenshot everything. Before you hand over any item in-game, take a screenshot of the agreed trade in the RellMarket chat. If something goes wrong, you'll have evidence to include in a report.
  • 🔄 Mark trades as completed. Once you've finished a trade in-game, come back to RellMarket and mark it as completed. This helps build both traders' reputation on the platform.

🔐 Protect Your Account

  • 🚫 Never share your Roblox password with anyone. Not with other traders, not in Discord, not anywhere. RellMarket staff will never ask for your password.
  • 📧 Use a real email address when signing up so you can verify your account and recover it if needed.
  • 🔑 Use a unique password for RellMarket. Don't reuse the same password you use for Roblox or other games.

🚨 How to Report a Listing or Trader

If you find a listing that's fake, misleading, or being used to scam people, you can report it directly from the item page. Look for the Report button near the listing details and describe what's wrong.

Our moderation team reviews all reports. Listings that break the rules will be removed and repeat offenders can be banned from the platform.

You can also reach out via our Discord server if you need to report something urgently or your account has been compromised.
