Terms of Service

By using RellMarket you agree to these rules. We've kept them short and easy to understand — no legal jargon.

🔞 Who Can Use RellMarket

You must be 13 years old or older to create an account and use RellMarket. This matches Roblox's own age requirement and applies regardless of where you live.

By signing up, you're confirming that you meet this requirement. If we find out an account belongs to someone under 13, we'll remove it.

📋 The Rules

These are the things you agree to when you use RellMarket:

  • 1 No scamming. Attempting to deceive another user, offering fake items, or backing out of agreed trades is not allowed and will result in a ban.
  • 2 Real in-game items only. Listings must be for actual Rell Seas in-game items or services. No real-money transactions, no off-topic listings, no fake items.
  • 3 No harassment. Be respectful to other traders. Sending abusive trade requests, spamming listings, or harassing users in any way will get your account banned.
  • 4 No inappropriate content. Profile pictures, usernames, listing descriptions, and any other content you post must be appropriate for all ages. Nothing offensive, sexual, or hateful.
  • 5 One account per person. Creating multiple accounts to avoid a ban or manipulate the platform is not allowed.
  • 6 Honest listings. Don't misrepresent what you're selling. If your listing says "Legendary Dragon Fruit", it should be a Legendary Dragon Fruit.
  • 7 No exploiting the platform. Don't attempt to abuse bugs, manipulate pricing data, or interfere with other users' trades.

🔨 Enforcement

If you break these rules, we may take any of the following actions depending on how serious the violation is:

  • Remove the listing or content that breaks the rules.
  • Issue a warning to your account.
  • Temporarily suspend your account.
  • Permanently ban your account with no right of appeal for serious violations (e.g. scamming).

You can report listings that break these rules using the Report button on any item page. We review all reports.

⚠️ What We're Not Responsible For

RellMarket is a fan-made platform that facilitates trades between players. We are not responsible for:

  • Items lost or scammed in trades that occurred outside of RellMarket.
  • In-game item values changing (the market is driven by players, not us).
  • Any actions taken by Roblox or the Rell Seas developers regarding your account or items.
  • Downtime or data loss (we do our best to keep the platform running reliably).

Use RellMarket at your own risk and always follow the safety tips on our Safety Tips page.

We may update these Terms of Service occasionally. If we make significant changes, we'll announce them via the platform. Continuing to use RellMarket after changes are posted means you accept the updated terms.
