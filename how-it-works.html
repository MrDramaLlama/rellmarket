How It Works

Trading on RellMarket is quick and straightforward. Here's everything you need to know to get started.

🗺️ Trading Step by Step

1

Sign in with Roblox

Sign in with your Roblox account. Click Log In and you'll be taken through Roblox's secure login. Your Roblox username becomes your RellMarket identity automatically.

2

Browse listings

Search by item name or filter by category, rarity, and price type. You can see who posted each listing, check their profile, and add anything you like to your Watchlist to keep track of it.

3

Send a trade request

Found something you want? Click the Trade button on a listing and describe what you're offering in return. The seller will get a notification and can accept or decline your offer.

4

Complete the trade in-game

Once both sides agree, meet up in Rell Seas and complete the trade. Then come back to RellMarket and mark the trade as completed. This builds your completed trades count on your profile.

🗺️ Browse Listings ＋ Post a Listing

🔨 How Auctions Work

Some sellers choose to run an auction instead of a fixed price. When you visit an auction listing you'll see the current highest bid, the minimum bid increment, and a countdown timer showing when it ends.

Place a bid with the amount you want to offer. If someone outbids you before the auction ends, you'll get a notification so you can decide whether to bid again. The highest bidder when the timer hits zero wins the item and completes the trade with the seller.

❤️ The Watchlist

See a listing you're interested in but not ready to trade yet? Hit the heart icon to save it to your Watchlist. You can review everything you've saved at any time from the Watchlist page.

This is useful for keeping an eye on items you're saving up Beli for, or waiting to see if a price drops.

🔍 Looking For Listings

Not selling anything? You can post a "Looking For" listing to tell the community what item you're hunting. Other traders can find it and send you a trade offer — you don't have to go searching yourself.

This is great for rare items that don't come up for sale often. Just post what you want and what you're willing to offer, and wait for someone to come to you.
